*The Legend of Zelda: Tears of the Kingdom* is a 2023 action-adventure game developed by Nintendo EPD for the Nintendo Switch. It is the twentieth game in [*The Legend of Zelda* series](@/notes/The_Legend_of_Zelda_media.md).
